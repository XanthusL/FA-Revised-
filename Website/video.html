Every week in Singapore, 40% of what we put in our recycling bins never actually gets recycled - not because we don't care, but because we're never quite sure what goes where. That's where GreenLens comes in. Just snap a photo of anything you're about to throw out, send it to our Telegram bot, and get instant, NEA-verified guidance on exactly how to dispose of it - no searching, no guessing. Whether it's a greasy pizza box, a broken phone, or something you've genuinely never had to sort before, GreenLens tells you the right bin, the right prep, and why it matters. Because recycling right shouldn't be a mystery. Recycle right, not just recycle.
